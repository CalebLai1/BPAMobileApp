About NECS 2026

The National Esports Championship Series (NECS) 2026 is an annual nationwide esports tournament bringing together the top competitive teams and players in the United States. The 2026 championship is hosted in Nashville, Tennessee from May 6–10, 2026, featuring elite competition in Valorant, Rocket League, and Super Smash Bros.
